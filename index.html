About Akal Academy

Akal Academy Canada is a registered non-profit organization and full-time Gurmat school established to promote Gursikhi and continue the mission of Sant Attar Singh Ji and Sant Teja Singh Ji.

We offer comprehensive religious education for children and youth, including youth camps, educational seminars, sports programs, and community outreach initiatives.
